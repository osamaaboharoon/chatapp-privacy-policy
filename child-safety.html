Child Safety Standards – Chat App

Last updated: 12 June 2025

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

Chat App strictly prohibits any content that exploits or endangers children. Users who share, request, or promote child sexual abuse material (CSAM) are immediately suspended and reported.

2. Reporting & Moderation

  • In-App Reporting: Users can report suspicious messages via the “Report” icon in every chat.
  • Manual Review: Reported messages are reviewed within 24 hours by our moderation team.

3. Law-Enforcement Cooperation

All verified CSAM is reported to the Egyptian National Council for Childhood & Motherhood (NCCM) and, where applicable, to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).

4. Data Retention

Content flagged as CSAM is preserved securely and shared only with lawful authorities during investigations.
