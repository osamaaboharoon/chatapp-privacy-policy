Privacy Policy for ChatApp

Last updated: 12 June 2025

ChatApp is a chat application developed by Osama Farrag (o.haroon111@gmail.com / 01154060470). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We collect and store the email addresses and display names used for login via Firebase Authentication. We also collect the device’s Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token to enable push notifications.

2. Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for enabling user login, identifying chat participants, managing messages using Firebase, and sending push notifications using FCM. Notifications are delivered securely through a self-hosted backend (Railway) using FCM. We do not sell, share, or expose your data to third parties.

3. Data Deletion

Users can request account deletion at any time. Deleting an account permanently removes all profile information and associated chat messages from Firebase.

4. How to Delete Your Account or Data

To request deletion of your entire account or specific chat data:

  1. Email us at o.haroon111@gmail.com with the subject line Account/Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the email address you used to register in the app.
  3. Specify whether you want to delete the whole account or just selected chat history.

Once verified, your requested data will be permanently removed from our servers within 7 business days.

5. Account Logout

Users can log out of the application at any time, which ends their session locally on the device.

6. Third‑Party Services

This app uses Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore). Firebase’s privacy policy is available at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

6.1 Push Notifications & Backend Server

ChatApp uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver push notifications about new messages. Your FCM token is stored securely in our Firebase Firestore database and used solely for notification delivery. We use a secure external backend server hosted on Railway to trigger and send these notifications via Firebase.
